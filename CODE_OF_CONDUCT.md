# Code of Conduct

MRIGraph adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in MRIGraph a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing scientific viewpoints and approaches
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the scientific community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**

- Harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Fabricating or manipulating scientific data or results
- Plagiarising code without attribution
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or academic setting

## Scientific Integrity

This project is associated with academic research. Contributors must:

- Not submit fabricated benchmark results or manipulated data
- Attribute prior work, related tools, and dependencies correctly
- Never commit real patient or subject neuroimaging data to this repository

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening a **private** GitHub Security Advisory or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
